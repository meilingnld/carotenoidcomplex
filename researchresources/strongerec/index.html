When the TAP of Carotenoid Complex was compared to Vitamin C, the results were equally impressive. One capsule of Carotenoid Complex equals the antioxidant power of two 200 mg tablets of vitamin C.

There simply is no other natural, food-based supplement on the market that has the antioxidant strength of Daily Carotenoid Complex.

* Total Antioxidant Potential (TAP) is a standardized, laboratory method of measuring the strength of an antioxidant. It allows one antioxidant to be compared to another on an equal basis.
